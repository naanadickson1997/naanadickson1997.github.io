Bio

Page under construction...I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at North Carolina State University. My research interests are in applied microeconomics, with a focus on education, industrial organization, gendered markets, and consumer products.

My current research examines school choice policy in Ghana and pricing patterns in over-the-counter supplement markets. I am also passionate about teaching and helping students develop clear, practical economic reasoning.

Research
